    How to play

    SCRAMBLED

    The word at the top is a random word at least 8 letters (possibly more)
    and it's all scrambled up!

    Can you unscramble it? Find the correct order to the letters and submit your guess!
    You'll be awarded 5 points for unscrambling it,
    and the letters above will take the correct order.

    Additionaly you can use the letters to make any word!
    You don't have to use all the letters, even just two letters, if its a word you can score 1 point!

    Having trouble unscrambling? need a hint?
    For the low cost of 2 points you can get a hint and it will reveal the placement of one letter.

    You can attempt the daily word which is the same for everyone and compare with your friends!
    Or hit random word to just play any random words!
